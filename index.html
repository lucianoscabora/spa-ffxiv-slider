Final Fantasy XIV Online, is a MMORPG video game developed by Square Enix exclusively for Microsoft Windows. It is set in Eorzea and players take control of a customizable avatar as they explore the world, getting caught up in both the invading Garlean Empire and the threat of the Primals, deities of the local tribes. Eventually they become involved in a plot by a Garlean legato to destroy the Primals by crashing one of the planet's natural satellites into Eorzea.
